Paws & Trails

Adventures in and around Alingsås, Sweden

The adventure guide to Alingsås that didn't exist,
so we made it.

Trails, gravel routes, fire spots, shelters, camp sites and good coffee stops, all handpicked by a German who made Alingsås home, and her four-legged co-explorer. We moved here, got wonderfully lost a few times, and decided to put everything we found on one map. Hike it, ride it, sit by a fire, grab a fika.

New spots get added as we explore.
